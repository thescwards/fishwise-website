Frequently Asked Questions

How do I identify a fish?

Tap the Scan tab at the bottom of the screen, then take a photo of the fish or select one from your gallery. Our AI will analyze the image and provide identification results within seconds.

How do I log a catch?

After scanning a fish, tap "Save Catch" to add it to your history. You can add details like weight, length, location, and notes. You can also view and edit your catches in the Catches tab.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device, find FishWise, and tap Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

Why isn't the fish being identified correctly?

For best results, make sure the photo is clear, well-lit, and shows the fish's distinctive features. Try taking the photo from a different angle or in better lighting. Our AI works best with clear, focused images.

Is my data backed up?

Your catches and trips are stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your iPhone, your app data may be included in your device backup.

How do I restore my subscription on a new device?

Open FishWise on your new device and go to the menu. Tap "Restore Purchases" and sign in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

Are the fishing regulations accurate?

We strive to keep regulations up to date, but they can change frequently. Always verify current regulations with your state's fish and wildlife agency before fishing.

Still need help?

Our support team is here to assist you. We typically respond within 24-48 hours.
