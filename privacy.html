Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

AI4U LLC ("we", "our", or "us") operates the FishWise mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information Collection

FishWise is designed with your privacy in mind. Here's what you should know:

  • Local Storage: Your catches, trips, and fishing data are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store this data on our servers.
  • Camera Access: We request camera access solely for fish identification. Photos are sent securely to Google Gemini AI for processing and are not stored on our servers.
  • Location Data: If you enable location services, we use this to provide fishing forecasts, weather conditions, and spot analysis. Your coordinates are sent to our weather and AI service providers to deliver localized information. Location data is not stored on our servers.

Contact Information

If you contact us through the in-app support form, we collect your email address and message content to respond to your inquiry. This information is used solely for customer support purposes.

Subscription Information

Subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple handles all payment processing securely. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status using an anonymous identifier that is not linked to your personal identity.

Third-Party Services

FishWise uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini AI: For fish identification and spot analysis. Images and location data are sent securely for processing.
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management using anonymous user IDs.
  • WeatherAPI: For fishing forecast and weather data. Location coordinates are sent to provide localized forecasts.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Since most data is stored locally on your device, your data security is largely dependent on your device's security settings. Data transmitted to third-party services is sent over secure, encrypted connections.

Children's Privacy

FishWise does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
